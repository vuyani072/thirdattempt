Everything You Need To Know About Isifuba

ISIFUBA MEDICAL SUPPLIES (PTY) LTD - a South African Medical Device Distribution Company established in 2013. We are a multidisciplinary medical distribution company with extensive knowledge of the South African healthcare environment, relationships with key opinion leaders (KOL’s), private hospital groups, the private insurance (reimbursement) industry and government sectors. The company has earned its reputation as a significant supplier of quality products and provider of professional services in both the public and private healthcare markets. Our business relationships are based on absolute integrity, leadership, vision and collaboration with strategic partners to help shape the healthcare environment in South Africa, and to deliver what our patients need. 100% Black Owned and 75% Black Woman Owned, a Level 1 BBBEE Rating with 100% black management, led by a female Managing Director.




OUR CORE COMPETENCIES INCLUDE

• Supply product solutions for our customers through collaboration

• Ability to train, develop and inspire people to deliver results.

• Being a trusted partner with the ability to deliver results.

• Ability to build long lasting relationships with people, through integrity honesty and empathy.

• Providing complete supply chain management procurement solutions for clients requiring goods sourced in South Africa and delivered into Africa.

MISSION

Develop high expertise and using top class medical devices in providing quality care to patients in partnership with our suppliers and professionals in the medical field.

VISION

To be a reliable partner in restoring family Health and improving quality of life beyond patients’ medical procedures.

SPECIALITIES

General Surgery, Cardiothoracic Surgery, Cardiology, Gynaecology, Urology, Bariatric Surgery, Oncology Product Portfolio, Access, Instruments, Energy Stapler

VALUES

Honesty, Integrity and Respect.
